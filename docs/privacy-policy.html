# Privacy Policy — Nāvisg **Last updated: May 26, 2026** ## 1. Introduction Nāvisg ("we", "our", or "the app") is a Singapore public transport information app that provides real-time bus arrivals, MRT/LRT information, carpark availability, and traffic incident data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data. ## 2. Data We Collect Nāvisg **does not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data to our servers. All data is processed locally on your device. ### 2.1 What the app accesses: - **LTA DataMall API** — called directly from your device to fetch public transport data. You provide your own API key, which is stored locally on your device and never sent to us. - **Location (optional)** — used only to show your current location on the map and find nearby bus stops. Location data never leaves your device. - **Internet** — required to fetch real-time transport data from LTA's public APIs. ## 3. Third-Party Services - **Google AdMob** — banner ads are served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can view Google's policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy. - **LTA DataMall** — public transport data is sourced from Singapore's Land Transport Authority. Your API key is stored locally and transmitted directly to LTA's servers. ## 4. Data Storage Your LTA API key and app preferences (favorite routes, language choice, map settings) are stored locally on your device using shared preferences. No data is stored on our servers. ## 5. Children's Privacy This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will investigate. ## 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. ## 7. Contact For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via GitHub: https://github.com/LIONBABYCRYPTO/navisg
